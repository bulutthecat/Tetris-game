Game themes:

- Tetris game
- Soviet Union / 8-bit style (pixel fireplace)
- budget / time managment aspects

Game idea:
- labor job (simulated through tetris difficulty)
- representation of food, and time which needs to be managed
- game gets harder as you play
- different endings
- 'levels' in the form of in-game 'years'

Gameplay mechanics:

- if you cant clear a row in time:
  > There is a penalty

- There is a timelimit, at the end, the level ends

- There are family members which you need to look after:
  > food / water
  > medicine
  > heating
